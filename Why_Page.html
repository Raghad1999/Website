Health

Veganism is great for health because it helps prevent numerous diseases for example cardiovascular disease and cholesterol.
It is also easier to have a nutritious lifestyle wsith vagn diets often being low in saturated fats and being high in fibre and vitamins.
Vegan lifestyles often leads to added benefits such as stronger hair, nails, reduced menstrual pains and increased weight loss.
For more information and statistics please visit the cowspiracy website.

Environment

Animal agriculture is the leading cause to the deforestation of the Amazon rainforest and uses 3/4 of the worlds water supply.
By becoming vegan people are reducing their carbon footprint, the amount of greenhouse gases they contribute to the atmosphere and the environment.
Vegans also require less land for their diets than meats (less than three times the amount) and therefore contribute to a more sustainable living.
