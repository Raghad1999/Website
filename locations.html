Vegan Restaurants: The Best In London, As Recommended By Vegans






Locations of Vegan Restaurants

We asked London's most influential vegans to recommend us their favourite vegan restaurants and cafe's.

Mildred's

Having graced Soho for almost thirty years, Mildreds is one of the best-loved vegan restaurants in town. They can count Blue Ollis amongst their many fans, who have helped Mildreds to spread to Camden, Dalston, and Kings Cross. Here, your plant-based food comes in the guise of Sri Lankan curries, halloumi burgers, and even peanut butter chocolate mousse cake. Whoever said eating clean was boring clearly never ate here.

fed by water

Brought to our attention by Secret London’s resident vegan Asli, Fed By Water is a modern Italian restaurant specialising in clean eating. Their commitment runs so deep that they use only purified water during the cooking process. So when you leave this place, you’ll be extra healthy – and stuffed full of delicious pasta, of course.

Purezza

As you may know from my Vegan Food Guide To Camden, Camden is pretty darn incredible for vegan food options. One of the latest companies to join the neighbourhood is entirely plant-based pizzeria, Purezza and I recently went along to check out their menu (and consume all of the pizza, obviously!).
